# Megamorphosis Private Launch Plan

## Goal

Bring in a small first cohort intentionally, observe whether Megamorphosis creates repeat progress behavior, and learn which loops are strong enough to scale.

## Ideal First Cohort

- 10-15 people total.
- People actively working on a real transformation, not casual browsers.
- A mix of categories: fitness, career, creative work, recovery, learning, and personal discipline.
- People willing to post at least three check-ins in the first week.
- People comfortable giving direct feedback.

## Invite Criteria

Invite someone if they can answer yes to at least two:

- They have a concrete goal they are already pursuing.
- They would benefit from visible accountability.
- They are comfortable sharing progress in a small community.
- They are likely to comment supportively on others' updates.
- They can test on mobile and desktop.

## Invite Message

Stephen

## First 7 Days

Day 0:

- Confirm Vercel deployment is healthy.
- Confirm Supabase migrations are applied.
- Confirm welcome email and daily digest are configured.
- Create or review seed Circles.
- Invite the first 5 users manually.

Day 1:

- Watch onboarding completion.
- Confirm users can post first check-ins.
- Check notifications, comments, and email logs.

Day 2-3:

- Invite the next 5-10 users.
- Encourage each user to follow at least three people or join one Circle.
- Comment on early posts to model the tone.

Day 4-5:

- Review which users returned without being prompted.
- Check whether Today is pointing to useful actions.
- Watch for moderation/reporting/blocking issues.

Day 6-7:

- Ask five users for feedback.
- Capture friction, confusing copy, missing actions, and moments of delight.
- Decide whether to keep iterating privately or invite another small cohort.

## Signals to Watch

Strong signals:

- Users complete onboarding without help.
- Users post more than one check-in.
- Users comment on someone else's progress.
- Users return from a notification or digest email.
- Users understand what a Journey is after seeing one example.

Weak signals:

- Users sign up but do not post.
- Users post once but never return.
- Users do not know what Circle to join.
- Users use DMs but not Journeys.
- Users ask what they are supposed to do next.

## Questions to Ask Users

- What were you trying to accomplish when you signed up?
- Did the first check-in feel natural or forced?
- Did Today give you a useful next action?
- Did you understand the difference between a Journey and a post?
- What would make you come back tomorrow?
- What felt too public, too private, or unclear?
- What would you tell a friend Megamorphosis is?

## Launch Guardrails

- Keep the first cohort small enough to personally observe.
- Do not optimize for vanity signup count.
- Do not add monetization until the return loop is clear.
- Keep moderation visible to the founder during private launch.
- Replace MVP legal/support language before broad public launch.
