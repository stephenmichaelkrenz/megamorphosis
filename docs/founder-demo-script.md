# Megamorphosis Founder Demo Script

## One-Liner

Megamorphosis helps people turn personal transformation into visible momentum through Journeys, proof posts, focused Circles, and supportive social accountability.

## 5-Minute Demo Flow

1. Start at the homepage.
   - Show the hybrid product surface: brand, public explanation, and live progress feed.
   - Say: "This is not a static goal tracker. It is a place where progress becomes visible."

2. Show signup and onboarding.
   - Create or describe a new account.
   - Complete profile setup.
   - Land on the welcome flow and post the first check-in.

3. Show the returning-user loop.
   - Open the dashboard or homepage while logged in.
   - Point to Today: check-in status, stale Journey prompt, Circle activity, unread notifications, and next best action.

4. Show a Journey.
   - Open an active Journey.
   - Highlight milestones, updates, Respect, comments, and visibility.
   - Explain that Journeys are the durable story, while posts are the daily proof.

5. Show Circles and social activity.
   - Open Discover or Circles.
   - Show following, Circle check-ins, comments, and DMs.
   - Explain that the app is built for accountability without making everything public by default.

6. Show trust and retention.
   - Open Notifications, Account Settings, and Help.
   - Mention reporting, blocking, moderation, email digest preferences, welcome email, and daily unread digest.

## Investor/Advisor Framing

- Problem: people start transformation efforts alone, lose momentum, and have no durable social proof of change.
- Product: Megamorphosis combines goal tracking, public proof, community accountability, and lightweight retention loops.
- Differentiator: the app is built around visible transformation narratives, not generic productivity tasks or passive social posting.
- Near-term path: private launch, observe user loops, refine onboarding, strengthen moderation/support, then add monetization around Pro features and premium Circles.

## Demo Close

"Megamorphosis is designed to make change visible. The MVP now has the core loop: start a Journey, post proof, receive support, return through Today and email, and stay protected through moderation and account controls."
